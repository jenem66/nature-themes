Guidelines

  1. Your artwork can be created as any type of medium (example: drawing, painting, poem, music, etc).
  2. All themes are based on mother nature
  3. Please make the selected theme prominent within each project.
  4. Post your work when you finish or by the due date on the blog. To do this, press the "Submit Work" button in the menu and enter your login information. Next, follow the guide in the following video tutorial: How to submit artwork
  5. Feel free to write anything about what inspired your work and include a link if you want to sell or include your social media page.
  6. The due date will be 30 days after the agreed start date of every project.
  7. We will take turns in alphabetical order to select a theme using the “Theme Generator”.
  8. The goal is to create art that is peaceful and calming.
  9. This club does not require critiquing of anybody's work. However, if there's something you admire, feel free to share!
